TL;DR

You don't need to read past this section unless you're just really interested in the numbers.

Against every NPC tested, the DPS was as follows (from best to worst): unnerfed dragon darts > unnerfed rune darts > unnerfed adamant darts > nerfed dragon darts > nerfed rune darts > nerfed adamant darts. This trend was obvious enough that I could've/should've stopped after, like… halfway through the inferno creatures or something, but I was too far in at that point.

Other observations/takeaways:

  • Nerfed blowpipe and dragon darts would put it just below where unnerfed adamant darts and unnerfed blowpipe are now: in most cases, it loses by one max hit (sometimes two) and <1 overall DPS
  • 3 to 5 max hits lost across the board depending on your darts and if you're on-task.

Proposed blowpipe nerfs and their effects on DPS

If you have any questions, comments, concerns, etc., feel free to message gig#4409 on Discord.

Context

Sometime earlier today, Jagex proposed the following nerfs to the blowpipe:

  • Ranged attack 60 -> 30
  • Ranged strength 40 -> 25

and the following nerfs to the darts that go in the blowpipe:

  • Adamant
    • Acc. 11 -> 7
    • Str. 10 -> 8
  • Rune
    • Acc. 15 -> 11
    • Strength remains the same
  • Dragon
    • Acc. 18 -> 15
    • Strength remains the same

(Bronze through mithril have proposed nerfs as well, but nobody uses those.)

This write-up (or whatever you want to call it) contains some DPS calcs that demonstrate how much these nerfs matter.

Thanks to Max for telling me what NPCs to run said calcs against and Bitterkoekje et al. for the DPS calculator.

How?

Nerfing the blowpipe in the DPS spreadsheet is pretty straightforward: just go to the "Items" sheet and change the ranged accuracy and strength values for the blowpipe and darts.

Inferno creatures

Off-task setup: blowpipe, justiciar faceguard, assembler, anguish, arma chestplate, arma chainskirt, barrows gloves, pegasians

On-task setup: same thing, replace faceguard with slayer helm

Assumes you're range potted and praying rigour, giving you 134/99 range.

Also, I don't think people usually take adamant darts to the inferno, but they're still included here for the sake of comparison.
